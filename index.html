Game Rules

Overview: Hook â€˜Em Poker is a 5-card poker game played with a standard 52-card deck, giving players two opportunities to form a winning hand. The game is played against a paytable, not against the dealer or other players. Players win if they receive a pair of Jacks or better.

Getting Started: Place a wager on the ante. Once all wagers are placed, the dealer will give each player 3 cards face down and then will place two cards face down in the first and second community card boxes.

Play or Fold: After seeing your initial 3 cards, decide to make a play bet wager of 1x to 5x your ante or fold. If you fold, your ante wager is forfeited.

Winning and Losing: Once the community cards are revealed, bets are settled. You win if your hand combined with either community box forms a pair of Jacks or better (pairs of 6s-10s push). The highest-ranking 5-card hand is used for payouts. Ante bets are paid out based off the paytable. Play bets are paid out 1:1 for any qualifying hand that isn't a push/loss

Paytable

  • Royal Flush: 500 to 1
  • Straight Flush: 150 to 1
  • Four of a Kind: 45 to 1
  • Full House: 15 to 1
  • Flush: 10 to 1
  • Straight: 8 to 1
  • Three of a Kind: 4 to 1
  • Two Pairs: 2 to 1
  • Pair of Jacks or Better: 1 to 1
  • Pair of 6s to 10s: PUSH
